Frequently Asked Questions

Is AniTube free to use?

Yes! AniTube is completely free. We believe anime should be accessible to everyone. You can browse, search, and join discussions without any cost.

How often is new anime added?

We update our library weekly with the latest episodes and series. Seasonal anime are added as soon as they air in Japan.

Can I request an anime that's missing?

Absolutely! Use our Contact Form to suggest titles. We prioritize popular requests.

How do I report inappropriate content?

Click the "Report" button under any content or message. Our mods review reports within 24 hours.

Are there age restrictions?

Some mature-rated anime require account verification. We comply with COPPA for users under 13.
